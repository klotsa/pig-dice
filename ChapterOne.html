Chapter One - A Curious Adventure


You find yourself wandering through the dense forest. You don't remember how you got here or where you are, but for some reason you feel compelled to follow the sun toward a tall rock formation. You climb atop and spot a wheathered old man on the other side. He is stooping to gather a drink from the glistening blue stream that carves through the forrest. His rags look as old as time and he is clearly struggling to gather the water in his hands. He sees you looking at him and narrows his eyes. "Whitless Boy!" he beckons you. "Would you answer my Riddle?"

Old Man's Riddle


Who would not be amazed by my strange lot? With my strength I bear a thousand forest oaks, But a slender needle at once pierces me, the bearer of such burdens; Birds flying in the sky and fish swimming in the sea Once took their first life from me; A third of the world is held in my power.

Riddle Solved


"Ah you must have heard this somewhere before. No matter, take this medicinal potion, it will come of use in your journey yet to come". You recieve the potion with gratitude and store it in your vestments for later use. The old man nods at you and shuffles off into the woods where he is no longer visible.

Riddle Failure


The old man shakes his head, laughing."May the gods forgive your moronic mind". He kicks you solidly with his foot. Your head collides with the stone formation behind you and everything goes black. When you open your eyes again you get an incredible sense of deja vu.
